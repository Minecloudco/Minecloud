Minecloud

> Connecting server owners to developers.

Welcome to Minecloud.

If you're a Minecraft server owner, you're in the right place. Minecloud is a development powerhouse that works for servers large and small.

What do we do?

At Minecloud we enjoy crafting plugins that will fit your gameplay and maximize your profits - and the fun level of your server. A Minecloud developer can differentiate your server from the masses. All of our code is designed to be completely fluid and readable, making it easy to make your own changes. We provide an extensive configuration file that will help you manipulate messages, interface, commands, and more.

How it Works

The Minecloud Team values your information, and we vow to keep everything you tell us confidential. If you are interested in working with us, we would be happy to assist you. In order to get in touch with us and book your project, please either add us on Skype, email us, or post on our Spigot thread. Please note that we do not accept anything but fixed price per-project arrangements. Perecentage offers, positions at your server, hourly rates, and other proposals will not be accepted under any circumstances. Thank you for understanding, and we hope to come to an agreement with you!
